# Sno-gelato-website
a small website about my best gelato place 

Flavor Showcase: A grid layout displaying a wide variety of gelato flavors, including classic Vanilla, Lotus, Pistachio, and Mix Berry, all priced at 85 LE.

Desserts & Cakes: A dedicated section for premium cakes (like Honeycomb Bowl and Caramelized Almonds) and treats like Cookies and Cinnabon.

About Us: Highlights the brand's commitment to authentic Italian craftsmanship and high-grade ingredients.

Multi-Location Info: Lists key branches across Egypt, including Maadi, New Cairo, Mivida, Tagamoa, and 6th of October.

Social Connectivity: Direct links to the brand's Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok profiles.
